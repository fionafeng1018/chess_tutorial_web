Joseki:

Joseki as known as formalized series of moves. In Go, joseki (or dìng shì in Chinese) are studied sequences of moves in the corner areas of the Go board, for which the result is considered balanced for both black and white sides. Because games typically start with plays in the corners, players often try to use their understanding of joseki to gain local advantages in the corners — advantages which can in turn make for a better overall position.
